Frequently Asked Questions

How do I activate my premium subscription?

After purchasing Premium, you'll receive a unique activation code. In ChatGPT, open the AlgoTutor widget and click "Activate Premium" in the header. Enter your code and your premium access activates instantly.

I lost my activation code. How do I recover it?

Go to your Dashboard and use the "Recover Your Premium Code" section. Enter the email you used to purchase Premium, and we'll retrieve your code.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription directly from the AlgoTutor widget in ChatGPT. Click the "Cancel Subscription" button in the top right corner of the widget. Your premium access will continue until the end of your current billing period.

Will I lose access immediately if I cancel?

No. When you cancel, your premium access continues until the end of your current billing period. You will not be charged again, but you can keep using premium features until that date.

What's the difference between Free and Premium?

Free users get 3 requests per day with access to all three modes (Learn, Build, Debug). Premium users get unlimited requests with the same modes, plus priority support.

How do I install AlgoTutor in ChatGPT?

Visit your Dashboard for step-by-step installation instructions. You'll need to add AlgoTutor from the ChatGPT Apps section in your settings.

Why isn't AlgoTutor working in ChatGPT?

Make sure you've added AlgoTutor as an app in ChatGPT (Settings > Apps > Add more apps > search "AlgoTutor"). Then start a new chat, click the "+" button, and select AlgoTutor under "More".
